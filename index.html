Ode to the Fading Light

by A. Willowscribe

The sun, a poet, dips its golden quill, To write its final line upon the hill. The ink of twilight, purple, soft, and deep, Awakens stars from their celestial sleep. A lonely oak, a silhouette so stark, Reads the last stanza of the fading park. And in the hush, a whispered, gentle breeze, Carries the silent stories of the trees.
